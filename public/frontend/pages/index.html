    Harvest

    The harvesting is done by hand, selecting only the ripe cherry-red fruits. This process requires experience and patience to ensure the highest quality.

    Manual collection grain by grain
    Only fruits at the optimal point of ripeness
    Season: April-June and October-December
    Yield: 40-60 kg of coffee cherries per day

    Benefit

    The beans are processed to extract the seed from the pulp. This includes depulping, fermentation, washing, and drying. This process defines a large part of the final flavor profile of the coffee.

    Pulping: separation of the pulp
    Fermentation: 12-24 hours in tanks
    Washing: removal of mucilage
    Drying: up to 10-12% moisture
